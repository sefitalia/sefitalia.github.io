The evolution of the business over 150 years of trade

Di Natale Family has been in the meat sector in Italy since 1850

Sefitalia srl – Broker has been in the meat sector in Europe since 1987


The web system “sefitalia.com” redefines business practices. In parallel to traditional trade it shifts the actiity into the Internet by expanding the borders all over the world, by enabling work in real time 24hours per day and by using a cross-sectoral archive system of 100.000 companies. sefitalia.com overcomes distances and time.
Sefitalia’s business is an “open business” : sefitalia is only a partner, and no form of speculation is allowed.


    The integrated B2B of Sefitalia is based on:

  • Purchase and Sale Service: business transactions and cocorded indirect exchange concluded rapidly with coverage all over the world;

  • Spot Transaction: two innovative sales/purchasing channels – targeted – confidential – discreet – screened. Tailored to our customers in the sectors related to the distribution of meat and food.

The supplyer deposit his pricelist in sefitalia.com: sefitalia will send it across the web, and will act on bhalf of the suppluer in a broker role or with an agreed triangulation.
The client enter in sfdtocks.com: see the pricelists, choose the products of interests and, within the site, accept or make an offer: without phone calls, without loss of time.
Sefitalia, along with the supplier and client, guarantee the communication and the resolution of the negotiation.
